Chocolate Cake Recipe:

Makes 16 servings

Ingredients:
- 3 cups all purpose flour
- 1 cup drinking chocolate
- 1 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/8 cup butter or oil
- 2 5/8 cup granulated sugar
- 3 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 3 eggs
- 1 1/2 cup water

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease baking pans.

2. Mix the flour, drinking chocolate, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together. Beat the butter sugar and vanilla extract in another bowl. Add eggs 1 at a time, mixing well. Beat flour mixture slowly, alternating with water. Spread into your prepared pans.

3. Bake for 25-35 minutes, or until a pick inserted in the center comes out clean. Let cool.

4. Frost with chocolate mousse.

Carrot Cake Recipe:

Makes 9 servings

Ingredients:

Cake:
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 1 1/2 eggs
- 1 cup all purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cup grated carrots
- 1/2 cup chopped/crushed walnuts

Cream Cheese Frosting:
- 4 tbsp unsalted butter
- 3/4 cup cream Cheese
- 1 1/2 cup confectioners sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
- A pinch of salt

1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and lightly flour a pan. Beat granulated sugar, oil, and eggs until blended. Add the flour, cinnnamon, baking soda, 1 teaspoon vanilla, and salt. Stir in the carrots and nuts. Pour into a pan.

2. Bake for 40-45 minutes for a rectangular pan, 30-35 minutes for a round pan. Cool completely.

3. Mix ingredients for cream cheese frosting and spread over your cooled carrot cake.

Vanilla Cake Recipe:

Makes 24 servings

Ingredients:

- 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
- 2 cups sugar
- 3 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- 2 large eggs
- 1 cup water

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. prepare a cupcake pan with liners.

2. Add the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt to a bowl and mix them together. Set the mixture aside.

3. Add the milk, vegetable oil, vanilla extract, and eggs to a medium sized bowl and mix.

4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and combine them.

5. Add the water to the batter part by part and combine well. Note: The batter should be very thin.

6. Fill cupcake liners about half-way and bake for 15-17 minutes. Cool cupcakes then frost with a frosting of your choice. Enjoy!

Blueberry Muffin Recipe:

Makes 8 muffins

Ingredients:

- 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/3 cup vegetable oil
- 1 egg
- 1/3 cup milk, or more as needed
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- 1 cup blueberries

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. Grease a muffin pan or line with muffin liners.

2. Mix the flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder. Put the vegetable oil into a 1 cup measuring cup. Crack the egg into the cup, and then fill with milk to fill the rest of the cup. Mix in with flour mixture. Fold blueberries in, and fill muffin cups.

3. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until they are done.

Chocolate Cookies(with drinking chocolate) Recipe:

Makes 24 cookies

Ingredients:

- 5/8 cup butter or margarine, softened
- 3/4 cup white sugar
- 1 large egg
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup all purpose flour
- 3/8 cup drinking chocolate
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/6 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 cup chopped walnuts

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.

2. Cream together butter and sugar until the mixture is smooth. Stir in eggs 1 at a time, then the vanilla. Mix the flour, drinking chocolate, baking soda and salt, and mix into wet ingredients. mix in walnuts. Put onto cookie sheets that are ungreased.

3. Bake for 8-10 minutes. Cool and enjoy eating!
